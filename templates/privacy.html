Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we store the expense records you create — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter into the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling filtering by date or category. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your expense data is stored on our servers and associated with your account. We take reasonable precautions to protect it, including access controls and secure connections.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third-party services. We use Google Fonts to load typography — this request is made directly from your browser to Google's servers and is subject to Google's own privacy policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channel. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
